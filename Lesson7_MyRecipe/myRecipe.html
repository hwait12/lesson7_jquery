Strawberry Orange Smoothies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla yogurt
  • 10 oz. frozen strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

Prepare blender.

Add yogurt, then strawberries and orange juice. Mix lightly.

Blend on medium until smooth.

If additional icey-ness desired, add 2-3 ice cubes and blend until smooth.

Repeat previous step until desired texture achieved.

Pour into glasses. Serves 3.
